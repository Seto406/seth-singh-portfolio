Project 2: Uncovering Insights (Data Science)

Building on the collected data, this project showcases the transition from engineering to analysis. By visualizing the time-series data, I identified key trends and built a predictive machine learning model to forecast future prices.

Annual Price Trends & Forecasting

Aggregating the data by year reveals clearer long-term trends. The final step was to train a linear regression model that achieved a high degree of accuracy in predicting future prices based on historical data.
